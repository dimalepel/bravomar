Our projects

Bravomar Holding has expertise within a niche market - coasters between 3,000 – 6,000 deadweight (dwt). Presently in cooperation with our long-standing partners Klip Marine Shipmanagement Ltd (technical ship management) and leading Scandinavian Charterers the vessels are being operated in the best interest of the investors.

Since 1995 the management of Bravomar Holding through associated companies has owned and managed more than 40 vessels.

Within 14 years of operation Bravomar Holding has successfully negotiated the vagaries of the economic and shipping cycles. All projects have been successfully concluded and with profits having been returned to investors. Current ongoing projects include:
