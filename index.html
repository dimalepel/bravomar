About Company

Bravomar Holding is a privately held project management company focused mostly on maritime-related investments since 2006.

Core strategy of Bravomar Holding is to identify profitable maritime opportunities, tailor-make projects and attract present and potential investors to set up business partnership.

The company provides various management services such as corporate, financial, technical, HR and commercial to the project companies through its associates or partnering companies. The unique selling point of Bravomar Holding is to bring all these activities together via a one-stop-solution. While many of these services are from 2006, technical and commercial management stem from 1990.

Services

  • Finding and evaluating a suitable vessel for potential investment project

  • Negotiating with Sellers and reputable Charterers

  • Designing and presenting transparent Business Plan for Investors

  • Administrating and Managing projects in professional and compliant manner in the best interest of Investors

  • Reporting efficiently and regularly to Investors and Authorities

  • Project Closing and Profit sharing
